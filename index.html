All about dogs

Your best friend

According to veterinary scientist Dr John Bradshaw, a dog will smile to persuade its owner to show it affection. Therefore, dogs are not always happy when they "smile" - they may even feel a little anxious and need reassurance. So go out and show your puppy how much you love him.

Dogs are the only animals capable of recognizing human emotions and empathizing with their owners. Dog nose prints are unique - no two are alike in the whole world.
